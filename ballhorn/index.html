In the Ballhorn lab, we are interested in interactions between organisms of different trophic levels including plants, herbivores as well as pathogenic and mutualistic microbes (fungi and bacteria). Furthermore, we study the effects of abiotic factors on these complex interactions. In our research, we combine laboratory approaches and field experiments to obtain a deeper functional understanding of these interactions in nature.
